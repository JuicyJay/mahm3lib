void pio_init ( pio_init_param *  param)

Initialize the clocks in the power management controller, neccecary for using digital_io_read_*.

Periphial IDs for PIO* PIO PID BIT #pins PIOA PID11 11 30 PIOB PID12 12 32 PIOC PID13 13 31 PIOD PID14 14 10 PIOE PID15 15 - PIOF PID16 16 -

Set bits 11 to 16 in PMC_PCER0 (0x400E0610) to enable clocks for a port To do this, first clear WPEN bit (bit 0) in PMC_WPMR (0x400E06E4)
